Introduction

Basliq is a simple project-management tool (in fact the world's simplest project management tool ever) which helps you to manage your work, have control over your tasks, orchestrate your team, or just make plans for your life. You can use Basliq from your modern browser.

caution

Basliq is still under development and doesn't have a stable and functioning release.
